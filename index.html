Why should you support local farmers?

Farmers have it hard in the Philippines. With typhoons regularly ravaging our country, and unpredictable weather patterns, they suffer greatly. Once they think they will have a bountiful harvest, a powerful storm will strike, ruining their hard work and their livelihood. Supporting them also positively affects our economy, boosting it. It also promotes sustainability, as we do not have to rely on imports for us to feed ourselves. Aside from that, buying produce directly from farmers is also cheaper than buying from a grocery or supermarket. So buying from farmers not only helps them out, but you're also helping yourself and saving your hard earned money.
